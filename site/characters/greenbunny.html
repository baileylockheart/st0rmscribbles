Ahh, you drew something new? I see, that's… How about you stick to music, okay?

They're the mean one. I'm not great at writing mean characters, but I KNOW they're the mean one. They come across as condescending to almost everyone they talk to, but ESPECIALLY Purple Cat. Generally speaking, they think childish = unintelligent, so that's where their perspective lies. Still, they're more than that - they love reading, and can get really into certain books and series. Still, if they don't think said series is either popular enough or profound enough to be taken seriously, they won't talk about it and might even use fabric covers to hide what they're reading. They're a consumer of all sorts of art, especially literature, but don't really know how to make it. This doesn't stop them from voicing their opinions on other peoples' art, of course, but as far as they're concerned, it comes from a place of appreciation.
Most of the time.
