Lym!!

Making websites isn't very profitable if you don't sell them, but hey, I can dream, right?

Lym was my first intentional attempt at making and drawing an OC with an actual design and color palette of some kind. They're not a sona or anything, she's based off of a stuffed animal I have! She's a web designer, and despite hating math with a passion, she's good at it, too. They've been making websites from an early age as a way to put her high intelligence to use at something she enjoys, rather than something they're forced to do. They're far from being burnt out, but they are running on steam half the time - a softer sort of pain, if you will. Still, they deal, and delve into the strange but entertaining dichotomy of making high-quality and modern-looking websites, all while listening to an eclectic selection of music through their iPod nano.
