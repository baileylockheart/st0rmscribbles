Sky!!

~ toyhouse ~

I have so many plots in my head that I could fuel a trilogy, but that won't stop me from making more of them.

Sky was designed for me by Gizmo_Doodles as an attack for Art Fight! I love this character very much, and I think they're super cool!!

Sky is quite a daydreamer, but they have several creative outlets that they can channel that through. Being both a bookworm and a musician, they create and consume various forms of art at all hours, so their appreciation of it runs deep. To say they get lost in their head can be an understatement, but at the same time, no one can blame them for it when the ideas that stem from those moments are often the most impressive. Pace around the kitchen with headphones on for twenty minutes, and suddenly, they're sitting down to plot out a novel. It's rare for them to finish such projects, not from a lack of ideas but rather from a surplus of them. They've constantly got too many beginnings and too few ends, but somehow, they see a song in that.
