Sunni!!

~ toyhouse ~

I don't know, I guess I just don't see the point in being able to go wherever I want and still not doing it. I dream of seeing the world, so that's exactly what I'm going to do.

Sunni was designed for me by Aloytea_ as an attack for Art Fight! I think this character super awesome, and I love them very much!!

Sunni is an adventurer at heart, and because of that, they're quite the wanderer. They maintain a practiced sense of curiosity, questioning and discovering new things wherever they go with an air of enjoyment, all while connecting it back to everything they've seen prior. This often leads them down rabbit holes of new questions, but they're all too happy to look for the answers. They're gentle by nature, having their adventures and leaving either without a trace or only leaving a positive one, but they know how to defend themself from the various dangers they may encounter as well. There's an interesting dissonance to their demeanor, both soft with childish innocence and rough around the edges from years of exploration, but this doesn't stop them from forging new connections and friendships everywhere they go.
